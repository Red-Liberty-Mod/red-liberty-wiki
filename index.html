Released December 31st, 2022

Red Liberty is an alternate history mod for Hearts of Iron IV, exploring a world in which the Socialist Party of America was victorious under the leadership of Eugene V. Debs, unseating the Republican Party in the political mainstream. Not all is well, however; the world stands at a precipice, with the Great War having ended in a stalemate, the Russian Civil War having stalled at the Urals, and the Austro-Hungarian Empire hanging on by a thread. Democratic Socialism has seen great successes, but it remains to be seen whether or not it will be enough to hold together a world on the brink of yet another catastrophic war.

About the Wiki

The Red Liberty Wiki is the central repository for canonical lore and background information related to the Red Liberty timeline. Articles are written from a (mostly) neutral, in-universe perspective as of 1936.
